Understanding the 80-20 RULE

The 80-20 rule, or Pareto principle, states that 80% of results come from

20% of efforts. It applies in areas like business and productivity,

highlighting the need to concentrate on the most impactful actions

for the best outcomes.

Why 80-20 in amazon?

In the busy Amazon marketplace, the 80-20 Amazon

Agency keeps things simple. We focus on the

essential 20% of actions that deliver 80% of results,

avoiding unnecessary complexity and noise.
