What is CVR?

  • CVR (Conversion Rate) on Amazon refers to the conversion rate, a metric used to measure the success of a campaign or strategy by determining the percentage of users or visitors completing a desired action, in our case, a sale.
  • The formula for calculating the conversion rate is:
  • CVR= (Total Number of Sales / Total Number of clicks) x 100

Key Factors Influencing Your CVR:

  • Your success on Amazon hinges on various factors:
  • 1. Main Image: A pivotal role in influencing CTR (Click-Through Rate); low conversion suggests customers opt for the competition.
  • 2. Communication within the Listing: Images, Title, Bullet Points, A+, Reviewsâ€”all crucial for clear communication targeting customer needs.
  • 3. Price: Competitively priced products fare better in CVR.
  • 4. Discount Coupons: An important feature enticing users to choose your product.

The Significance of a Strong CVR on Amazon:

  • A competitive CVR is essential for these reasons:
  • 1. Reduced PPC Campaign Spending: A good CVR means less spending on PPC campaigns.
  • 2. Increased Profitability: A good CVR leads to lower ACOS and higher profitability.
  • 3. Enhanced Visibility: Amazon rewards products with high CVRs by displaying them more frequently, boosting overall visibility.
